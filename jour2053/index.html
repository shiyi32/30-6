What is Santan

Named after Malaysia's beloved 'coconut milk', SANTAN is a reflection of the purity and simplicity of ASEAN flavours and ingredients. Inspired to share our love for ASEAN dishes and flavours with the world, SANTAN aims to uncover the best culinary secrets from ASEAN and bring this to you.
